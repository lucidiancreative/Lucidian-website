Dan Jones

Founder / Developer

Daniel is the founder of Lucidian Creative, where he bridges creative storytelling with AI-driven software development. As a full-stack creative developer, he builds animation tools and consumer apps—including Cinestorm (generative storytelling platform), Game Date (privacy-first dating app), and Moji (visual code enhancement). His work spans from custom ComfyUI nodes to React Native development, always with an eye toward making technical tools serve creative vision. Currently in production on BlueBoy, an animated series exploring identity and connection through the lens of AI-generated animation.
